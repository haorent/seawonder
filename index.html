A Hub for Wonders underwater

Seawonder is based on the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 14), which is Live Below Water. It is focused on conserving and sustainably using the oceans, seas, and marine resources.

Fun fact: Oceans cover more than 70% of our planet and are home to a vast variety of life that supports global biodiversity, regulates the climate, and provides food and livelihoods for billions of people.

However, pollution, overfishing, and climate change are threatening marine ecosystems. Protecting life below water means reducing plastic waste, promoting sustainable fishing, and conserving marine habitats to ensure a healthy and thriving ocean for future generations.

In this page, you will be discovering wonderful sea creatures below the sea.
