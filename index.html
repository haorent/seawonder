A Hub for Wonders Underwater

Seawonder is based on the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 14), which is Live Below Water. It focuses on conserving and sustainably utilising the oceans, seas, and marine resources.

Fun fact: Oceans cover more than 70% of our planet and are home to a vast variety of life that supports global biodiversity, regulates the climate, and provides food and livelihoods for billions.

However, pollution, overfishing, and climate change are threatening marine ecosystems. Protecting life below water means reducing plastic waste, promoting sustainable fishing, and conserving habitats.

In this page, you will discover wonderful sea creatures below the sea.
